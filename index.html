Our Services

Tile & Grout Cleanings

Using our 11-step process tile cleaning process, we will guarantee a clean job for a happy home!

Our process is as goes
  1. Clean Entry Way
  2. Pre-Vacuum
  3. Install Corner Guards
  4. Pre-Condition Frequent Areas
  5. Power Scrub Tile Surface & Grout Lines
  6. Hot Water Extraction with Neutral Water Rinse
  7. Power Vacuum Excess Moisture
  8. Steam Clean Baseboard
  9. Mop-dry Tile & Grout
  10. Turbo Dry
  11. Apply Tile & Grout Sealer

All cleanings are done with eco-friendly and pet-friendly chemicals, so you can rest easy, even after the job is done!

Carpet Deep Cleans

Using our 12-step process carpet cleaning process, we guarantee your carpet will be like new once again!

Our process is as goes
  1. Clean Entry Way
  2. Pre-Vacuum
  3. Install Corner Guards
  4. Pre-Condition Frequent Areas
  5. Spot-Treat Heavily Soiled Areas
  6. Power Scrub Problem Areas
  7. Hot Water Extraction & Neutral Water Rinse
  8. Power Vacuum Excess Moisture
  9. Steam Clean Baseboards
  10. Groom Carpet
  11. Turbo Dry Carpet
